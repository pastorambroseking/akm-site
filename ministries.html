Our Ministries

Kingdom Love Outreach

Dedicated to serving the homeless, orphans, widows, and those in need. We organize food distributions, shelter visits, and evangelism outreach to share the love of Christ in practical ways.

Healing School

A program designed to teach, mentor, and activate believers in divine healing, walking in faith, and demonstrating the power of God.

Men’s Coaching & Mentorship

Focused on building Kingdom men with purpose, identity, and leadership through coaching, resources, and accountability.

Digital Media & Teaching

Through ebooks, podcasts, YouTube, and social media, we spread the Gospel globally, reaching lives far beyond our physical location.
