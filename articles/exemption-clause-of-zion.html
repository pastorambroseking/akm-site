The Exemption Clause of Zion

Kingdom Reality • ~5 min read

Scripture shows patterns of divine exemption—like Goshen in Egypt and the promises of Psalm 91. In Christ, we live from Zion’s jurisdiction: a higher government that preserves, guides, and assigns us purpose.

Markers of Exemption

  • Presence: Nearness to God distinguishes His people (Exodus 33:16).
  • Promise: “No evil shall befall you” as you dwell in the secret place (Psalm 91).
  • Purpose: Exemption isn’t isolation; it’s empowerment to serve and bless.

Practicing Zion Consciousness

  1. Daily consecration and thanksgiving.
  2. Decrees from your seated position in Christ (Eph. 2:6).
  3. Walk in love, wisdom, and obedience.
“I dwell in the secret place of the Most High; I am preserved for purpose.”
