Scroll Alignment: Living by Heaven’s Record

Discipleship • ~4 min read

God’s purposes for our lives are written (Psalm 139:16). Scroll alignment is the practice of agreeing with that record and walking it out daily.

Three Anchors

  • Word: Scripture is the primary witness and boundary.
  • Spirit: Guidance, correction, and empowerment.
  • Witness: Fruit, peace, and wise counsel confirm direction.

Simple Daily Practice

  1. Morning: Present yourself to God; ask for alignment.
  2. Midday: Reset—discard distraction; receive clarity.
  3. Night: Close the day; seal what bears fruit.
“I align with Heaven’s scroll. I walk in clarity, courage, and obedience.”
