The Precious Blood of Jesus

Covenant • ~5 min read

The blood of Jesus is our covenant covering — it speaks better things than the blood of Abel (Hebrews 12:24). It cleanses, protects, and seals us into a new and living way.

What the Blood Does

  • Cleanses: Removes guilt and shame; we walk in the light (1 John 1:7).
  • Protects: We overcome by the blood and by our testimony (Revelation 12:11).
  • Seals the New Covenant: A finished work that grants peace with God (Ephesians 2:13–14).

How to Apply

  1. Thank God for the Blood of Jesus.
  2. Verbally “apply” the blood over your life, family, work, and ministry.
  3. Declare victory and stand in faith.
“By the blood of Jesus, I am redeemed, protected, and at peace.”
